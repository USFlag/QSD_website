About Us

Quality Sign Designer is a dynamic interactive flag, sign and banner design tool that enables its users to create stunning advertising display items without the need of incurring expensive and time consuming graphic design services. When you use Quality Sign Designer you will have a full complement of high speed large format ink jet technology at your finger tips including:

Three 10 1/2 ft wide roll to roll six color process digital printers designed to print flexible substrates such as reinforced vinyl sheeting for banners, billboards, trade show graphics, building murals as well as pressure sensitive vinyl, etc.

One giant 5'x10' 6 color process flatbed rigid products digital printing press that prints spectacular full color graphics directly to a variety of rigid and flexible substrates including: coroplast, foamcore, Sintra, Alumacore, MDO at a rate up to 800 SF per hour.

A new state of the art 10 1/2 ft wide high speed roll to roll 6 color process digital printing press, that prints water based dye directly to a full range of textiles primarily for the purpose of producing dramatic one to full color flags, high end banners and display products.

Since you will be designing, purchasing and simultaneously approving the design of your advertising concept, lead times will be drastically reduced and you will be in complete control of your advertising display products from inception to installation.
